Li Gong (Xuetao) holds a civil engineering degree and has spent many years as a veteran software developer — a rare combination. Most software engineers, when they talk about "engineering management," can only reference the software industry itself. In AgentsZone Episode 13, Li drew on civil engineering's standards, supervision systems, change management, and cost control to offer a fresh lens on software engineering in the AI era. He is dedicated to bringing the high-quality standards of the construction industry into software development.
